About the modern garbage bin

Modern Bin aims to improve people's health, safety, and quality of life worldwide. This innovative, self-sustaining, intelligent trashcan is the answer to the daily littering problems plaguing cities worldwide. That it is free is the cherry on top. Completely and utterly self-sufficient It has the potential to be a massive hit because it has the best team of people working on it. those with a lot of experience

Problems

  • Lack of public awareness on the importance of waste segregation and littering.
  • Lack of use of technology to provide seamless and effective solution to solid waste management problems.
  • Lack of segregation at source which leads to land fills.
  • Unable to recycle and re-use due to lack of segregation.
  • Black spots piling up in commercial areas and private properties.
  • Unfriendly vibe from dirty dumpsters and dustbins.

Our Solutions

  • Convenient proximity between bins for effective dumping.
  • Public awareness through advertisement, marketing strategy and campaigns in public and private properties i.e. schools, corporate offices etc.
  • Use of up to date technology for effective solutions for maintenance, security, safety and information.
  • Direct real-time information survey to public and government alike.
  • Aesthetically beautiful and user friendly design.

Mission

  • Make our products the most convenient, self-sufficient, innovative, intelligent bins to revolutionize trash management and how people view garbage bins.
  • Increase awareness of the critical nature of one's responsibilities regarding garbage disposal.
  • Collaborate with corporations and governments to effect positive change for future generations.
